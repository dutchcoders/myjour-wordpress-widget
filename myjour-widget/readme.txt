Installation:

1. Extract this zip on wp-content\plugins folder.
2. Then go to ' SITE_URL/wp-admin/plugins.php ' & find 'Myjour Widget' plugin & click on 'Activate' link.
3. After installation, go to ' Settings -> Myjour Widget' submenu of Admin Panel OR "SITE_URL/wp-admin/options-general.php?page=myjour_widget", here more details are available.
